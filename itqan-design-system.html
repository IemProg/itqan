Design System & Visual Language
01 — Design Philosophy
Sacred Minimalism

Itqan's design language draws from the Islamic tradition of finding beauty through precision and restraint. Just as Islamic geometric art creates infinite complexity from simple shapes, our interface creates a rich experience from minimal elements.

The dark canvas represents the quietude of study. Gold accents honor the tradition of gilded Quran manuscripts. Every element serves the sacred text — never competing, always elevating.

Principles
Reverence
The Quran text is always the hero. UI recedes.
Calm
No visual noise. Deep breathing room. Focused.
Precision
Every pixel, every spacing, every transition — intentional.
Warmth
Encouraging, never judgmental. Gold radiates warmth.

08 — Light Mode (Phase 2)
Parchment & Ink

The primary experience is dark mode — it reduces eye strain during long recitation sessions and evokes the focus of a dimly lit study room. However, a light mode will be offered in Phase 2 for outdoor use and user preference.

Light mode draws from aged parchment and traditional manuscript aesthetics — warm cream backgrounds, deep ink text, with the same gold accents.
